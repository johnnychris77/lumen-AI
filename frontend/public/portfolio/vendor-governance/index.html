Vendor Governance Workflow

1. Vendor Signal

Vendor quality signal is captured from tray, device, documentation, IFU, or site-specific issue.

2. Governance Review

Signal is categorized by vendor, risk level, site, owner, and device or tray.

3. CAPA Linkage

Vendor event can trigger or link to a CAPA record for corrective and preventive action.
