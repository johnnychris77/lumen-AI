# 1. Proposal Purpose

This proposal is intended to define a limited, structured LumenAI pilot for a qualified healthcare organization.

The pilot is designed to validate whether LumenAI can help the customer improve governance visibility across:

- Sterile processing quality trends
- CAPA recurrence and aging
- Vendor accountability
- Power BI and analytics readiness
- Executive reporting and decision support

The proposal is designed for early commercial pilot discussions after the customer has completed outreach, demo review, discovery, and pilot qualification.

# 3. Executive Summary

LumenAI is a healthcare governance intelligence platform designed to help leaders identify and review sterile processing quality trends, CAPA recurrence, vendor accountability signals, and executive reporting gaps.

This proposed pilot will evaluate whether LumenAI can support the customer’s governance visibility needs using a limited, non-PHI pilot model.

The pilot will focus on workflow validation, use case fit, reporting usefulness, stakeholder feedback, and executive review readiness.

---

# 4. Customer Problem Statement

Based on discovery, the customer is evaluating LumenAI because of one or more of the following challenges:

- Manual quality or CAPA reporting burden
- Recurring sterile processing quality events
- Limited visibility into CAPA recurrence or aging
- Vendor tray or vendor accountability concerns
- Fragmented reporting across sites or departments
- Limited executive visibility into operational risk trends
- Need for Power BI-ready governance reporting
- Need for structured pilot evidence and decision support

# 5. Proposed Pilot Objective

The objective of the pilot is to determine whether LumenAI provides useful governance intelligence for the customer’s operational, quality, vendor, analytics, and executive stakeholders.

The pilot will assess:

- Workflow fit
- Stakeholder usefulness
- CAPA governance value
- Vendor governance value
- Power BI / analytics readiness
- Executive review usefulness
- Non-PHI pilot feasibility
- Commercial expansion potential

---

# 6. Proposed Pilot Scope

## In Scope

The pilot may include:

- One or more sterile processing quality workflows
- CAPA recurrence and aging review
- Vendor accountability review
- Power BI export and data dictionary review
- Executive dashboard or summary review
- Weekly or biweekly governance check-ins
- Final executive pilot review package

## Out of Scope

The pilot will not include unless separately approved:

- Live PHI
- Direct production EHR integration
- Direct production Censitrac integration
- Direct production ERP integration
- Automated clinical decision-making
- Replacement of existing source systems
- Full enterprise deployment
- Long-term production support

# 8. Pilot Use Cases

## Use Case 1 — Executive Governance Visibility

Purpose:
Evaluate whether LumenAI helps leaders see quality, CAPA, vendor, and risk signals in an executive-ready format.

Success indicators:
- Leaders understand the summary
- Risks and aging items are easier to identify
- Executive review output is useful
- Follow-up priorities are clearer

---

## Use Case 2 — CAPA Governance Intelligence

Purpose:
Evaluate whether LumenAI helps identify CAPA recurrence, aging risk, owner accountability, and escalation needs.

Success indicators:
- CAPA recurrence is easier to see
- Aging risk is clearer
- Owner and due date tracking is useful
- Escalation logic is understandable

---

## Use Case 3 — Vendor Governance Intelligence

Purpose:
Evaluate whether LumenAI helps identify repeat vendor events, high-risk vendor patterns, CAPA-linked vendor activity, and business review readiness.

Success indicators:
- Repeat vendor issues are easier to review
- Vendor accountability is clearer
- CAPA-linked vendor activity is useful
- Business review preparation is improved

---

## Use Case 4 — Power BI / Analytics Readiness

Purpose:
Evaluate whether LumenAI’s export structure, data dictionary, and reporting fields support analytics and executive dashboard review.

Success indicators:
- Export structure is understandable
- Fields are relevant
- Data dictionary supports review
- Power BI dashboard use cases are clear
