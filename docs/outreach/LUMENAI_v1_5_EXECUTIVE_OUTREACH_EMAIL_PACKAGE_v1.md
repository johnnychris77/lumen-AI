The purpose of this package is to support the first structured outreach motion for LumenAI v1.5.

These emails are designed to:

- Open executive conversations
- Request feedback and discovery calls
- Introduce LumenAI as a pilot-ready governance intelligence platform
- Position LumenAI as non-PHI pilot friendly
- Invite stakeholders to review the hosted demo
- Move interested contacts toward pilot qualification

# 2. Messaging Principles

Use these principles across all outreach:

- Lead with leadership pain, not product features
- Keep the email short
- Ask for feedback before asking for a sale
- Emphasize non-PHI pilot readiness
- Focus on SPD quality, CAPA visibility, vendor accountability, and executive reporting
- Offer a brief 20-minute review
- Make the next step simple

# 17. Outreach Personalization Guide

Personalize each email with one or two of the following:

- Their role
- Their hospital or health system type
- Known surgical services or SPD focus
- Quality or CAPA responsibilities
- Vendor governance responsibilities
- Analytics or Power BI responsibilities
- A warm connection
- A specific reason they may care about governance visibility

Avoid:

- Overly technical detail
- Claims of proven customer outcomes before validation
- PHI language that implies production data access
- Long product descriptions
- Pressure to buy
