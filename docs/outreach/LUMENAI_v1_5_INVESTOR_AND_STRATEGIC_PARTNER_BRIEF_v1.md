# 2. Market Problem

Healthcare leaders often lack a structured governance layer across sterile processing quality events, CAPA follow-up, vendor accountability, and executive reporting.

Common gaps include:

- Recurring SPD quality issues are difficult to trend
- CAPA recurrence and aging may be tracked manually
- Vendor issues often sit in emails, spreadsheets, or isolated reports
- Executive leaders may not see risk patterns until escalation is needed
- Power BI or Excel reports may show activity without clear governance context
- Multi-site operations often lack standardized visibility across sites
- Pilot evidence and decision support are often fragmented

LumenAI addresses this by organizing operational, quality, vendor, and reporting signals into a structured governance intelligence model.

---

# 3. Product Positioning

LumenAI is positioned as a healthcare governance intelligence layer.

It is designed to help hospitals and health systems evaluate:

- SPD quality trends
- CAPA recurrence
- CAPA aging risk
- Vendor accountability signals
- Power BI export readiness
- Executive governance summaries
- Pilot evidence
- Commercial conversion readiness

LumenAI is not positioned as a replacement for core hospital systems. It is positioned as a decision-support and governance visibility layer that can begin with a low-burden, non-PHI pilot.
