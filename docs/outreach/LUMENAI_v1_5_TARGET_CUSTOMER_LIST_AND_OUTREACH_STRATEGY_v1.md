# 3. Ideal Customer Profile

## Best-Fit Organizations

LumenAI is best positioned for organizations with:

- Multi-site sterile processing operations
- High surgical volume
- Complex vendor tray workflows
- Recurring quality events
- Manual CAPA tracking
- Limited executive visibility into SPD quality trends
- Fragmented Power BI or Excel reporting
- Need for vendor accountability
- Leadership interest in governance visibility
- Interest in non-PHI pilot validation

## Strongest Initial Targets

The strongest initial targets are:

- Health systems with 3 or more hospitals
- Hospitals with centralized or offsite reprocessing centers
- Systems with active perioperative transformation work
- Systems with known SPD staffing or quality challenges
- Systems that use Power BI or enterprise analytics
- Systems open to pilot innovation without PHI integration

# 6. Outreach Prioritization Model

Each target should be scored from 1 to 5.

| Criteria | Score Meaning |
|---|---|
| SPD complexity | Multi-site, high volume, vendor-heavy operations |
| Executive relevance | COO, VP, or executive sponsor likely to care |
| Problem urgency | Known pain around quality, CAPA, vendor issues, or reporting burden |
| Pilot feasibility | Non-PHI pilot possible with limited implementation burden |
| Warm access | Existing contact, relationship, or credible introduction path |
| Commercial potential | Budget, scale, or expansion potential |

## Priority Interpretation

| Total Score | Outreach Priority |
|---:|---|
| 25 to 30 | Tier 1 immediate outreach |
| 19 to 24 | Tier 2 near-term outreach |
| 13 to 18 | Tier 3 nurture |
| Below 13 | Do not prioritize yet |

# 8. Recommended First Outreach Targets

## Tier 1 — Warm Network Targets

Use first where credibility is strongest.

Examples:
- Existing healthcare operations relationships
- SPD or perioperative leaders known through professional network
- Health system leaders familiar with SPD challenges
- Quality or surgical leaders familiar with governance reporting
- Trusted former colleagues or industry contacts

Outreach angle:
Ask for feedback, not a sale. Position LumenAI as a pilot-ready governance intelligence platform.

---

## Tier 2 — Strategic Health System Targets

Use after warm network outreach begins.

Examples:
- Multi-hospital systems with complex surgical operations
- Regional systems with shared SPD or market-level leadership
- Systems with active digital transformation or Power BI analytics programs
- Systems with offsite reprocessing or central sterile standardization work

Outreach angle:
Position LumenAI as a low-burden, non-PHI pilot opportunity for SPD quality, CAPA, vendor, and executive governance visibility.

---

## Tier 3 — Strategic Partners

Use in parallel with customer outreach.

Examples:
- Healthcare operations consulting groups
- Perioperative consulting firms
- Quality improvement consultants
- Healthcare AI advisors
- Analytics implementation partners

Outreach angle:
Position LumenAI as a partner-ready pilot execution framework that can support healthcare transformation work.

# 9. Outreach Messaging Themes

## Executive Theme

LumenAI helps leadership see SPD quality, CAPA, vendor, and governance signals in a structured executive review format.

## SPD Leadership Theme

LumenAI helps SPD leaders reduce manual reporting burden and show leadership where quality, vendor, and CAPA risks are recurring.

## Quality Theme

LumenAI helps quality leaders see recurrence, aging, escalation, and evidence-backed CAPA follow-up.

## Supply Chain Theme

LumenAI helps vendor governance teams track repeat events, high-risk vendors, CAPA-linked vendor activity, and business review readiness.

## Analytics Theme

LumenAI provides Power BI-ready export structure, data dictionary support, and executive dashboard validation.

## Pilot Theme

LumenAI can begin with a non-PHI pilot focused on governance visibility, workflow validation, and executive value.

# 11. Outreach Sequence

## Step 1 — Initial Outreach

Goal:
Introduce LumenAI and request a brief feedback or discovery conversation.

Timing:
Day 0

## Step 2 — Follow-Up

Goal:
Reinforce value and offer demo.

Timing:
Day 5 to 7

## Step 3 — Value Follow-Up

Goal:
Share one specific use case such as CAPA recurrence, vendor accountability, or Power BI executive visibility.

Timing:
Day 12 to 14

## Step 4 — Close or Nurture

Goal:
Ask whether the contact is open to a pilot discussion or should be revisited later.

Timing:
Day 21

# 14. Risk and Mitigation

## Risk 1 — Outreach too broad

Mitigation:
Prioritize warm network and high-fit targets first.

## Risk 2 — Message too technical

Mitigation:
Lead with leadership pain: governance visibility, reporting burden, CAPA risk, vendor accountability.

## Risk 3 — Security concerns slow pilot

Mitigation:
Lead with non-PHI pilot boundary and demo environment.

## Risk 4 — No clear buyer

Mitigation:
Map both executive sponsor and functional owner before proposing pilot.

## Risk 5 — No commercial path

Mitigation:
Use pilot qualification scorecard before investing time in proposal.
