## Renewal Readiness Checklist

### 30 Days Before Renewal Conversation

- [ ] Customer Health Score is Green (≥ 70) or trend is improving
- [ ] ROI report generated and reviewed with customer (`/roi-center`)
- [ ] Executive Command Center demo refreshed with live data
- [ ] SPD Director has seen Surgical Readiness and Global Registry dashboards
- [ ] At least one CAPA has been opened and closed (demonstrates workflow value)
- [ ] Baseline coverage ≥ 75%

### 15 Days Before Renewal Conversation

- [ ] QBR deck prepared using ROI Center export
- [ ] Expansion use cases identified (additional department, additional facility, vendor tier)
- [ ] Customer reference story drafted (with customer permission)
- [ ] Contract renewal terms confirmed with LumenAI finance

### Day of Renewal Conversation

- [ ] Open at `/executive-command-center` to show live KPIs
- [ ] Walk Section 2 of QBR template (ROI Summary)
- [ ] Present one forward-looking capability (Global Registry network intelligence roadmap)
- [ ] Confirm renewal or document objections

## Renewal Objection Handling

| Objection | Response | Supporting Evidence |
|-----------|----------|-------------------|
| "We're not sure the AI is accurate" | AI assists — it doesn't replace. All findings require human review. Accuracy improves with approved baselines. | Baseline coverage %, confidence scores on each finding |
| "We haven't used it much" | Yellow/Red health score — offer targeted training and workflow remediation. Address root cause. | Customer Health Score, adoption rate |
| "Too expensive" | ROI Center: time saved + critical findings avoidance. Calculate months to break-even. | ROI CSV export |
| "Joint Commission hasn't asked for this" | Proactive compliance posture. AORN ST91:2021 recommends documented reprocessing outcomes. | Audit evidence bundle |
| "Our vendor hasn't submitted baselines" | We can assist with vendor onboarding. Offer CS-facilitated vendor call. | Baseline coverage, vendor submission status |
| "We're moving to a different EHR" | LumenAI is SPD-specific, not EHR-replacement. Complements your EHR investment. | Module independence |

## Expansion Playbook

### Hospital Tier → Enterprise Tier
**Trigger:** Customer has > 1 facility interested, or usage approaching Hospital Tier limits  
**Value proposition:** Centralized governance across facilities, cross-facility benchmarking (Phase 20), network intelligence  
**Steps:**
1. Show Global Registry page as preview of multi-facility capability
2. Present Subscription Readiness tier comparison
3. Propose Enterprise Tier with current usage baseline

### Add Vendor Tier Seat
**Trigger:** Customer wants vendor to manage own baseline submissions  
**Value proposition:** Vendor self-service reduces CS burden, accelerates baseline coverage  
**Steps:**
1. Confirm vendor contact and email
2. CS provisions vendor tier account
3. Walk vendor through image capture guidelines

### Expand SPD Departments
**Trigger:** Customer has additional departments (endoscopy, ortho, cardiac)  
**Steps:**
1. Identify department lead
2. CS facilitates department-specific training session
3. Update instrument registry with new fleet

## Metrics to Bring to Renewal Conversation

Pull from `/roi-center` export. Target thresholds for a strong renewal conversation:

| Metric | Strong | Adequate | Weak |
|--------|--------|----------|------|
| Inspections completed | ≥ 200 | 50–199 | < 50 |
| Critical findings detected | ≥ 5 | 1–4 | 0 |
| Time saved (hrs) | ≥ 30 | 10–29 | < 10 |
| Estimated cost avoidance | ≥ $25k | $5k–$25k | < $5k |
| Baseline coverage | ≥ 80% | 50–79% | < 50% |
| Customer Health Score | Green | Yellow | Red |
