# LumenAI Enterprise Governance Suite Investor One-Pager v1

## Company / Product
**LumenAI** is building an enterprise quality intelligence platform for healthcare sterile processing, surgical services, infection prevention, quality, and vendor governance.

The platform connects frontline quality evidence, audit signals, corrective action workflows, portfolio evidence, and executive reporting into one validated governance layer.

---

## The Problem

Healthcare quality and sterile processing teams often operate with fragmented systems, manual evidence collection, delayed reporting, and limited executive visibility.

Common gaps include:

- Audit findings are difficult to connect to corrective action.
- Quality signals may be captured but not converted into accountable follow-up.
- CAPA workflows are often manual or disconnected from frontline evidence.
- Leadership lacks a real-time governance view of high-value events.
- Evidence packages for audits, executives, vendors, and stakeholders are time-consuming to produce.
- Data is often not ready for Power BI, executive dashboards, or portfolio-level review.

---

## The Solution

The LumenAI Enterprise Governance Suite creates a traceable pathway from audit visibility to corrective and preventive action.

The validated workflow is:

**Audit Signal → High-Value Event → CAPA Trigger → Owner Assigned → Corrective Action → Preventive Action → Governance Summary**

This moves LumenAI beyond inspection support and into enterprise quality governance.
