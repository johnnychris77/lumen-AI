# LumenAI Executive Story Mode

## Demo Scenario

A sterile processing technician identifies suspected retained debris inside a Frazier suction during borescope inspection. The finding is captured as visual evidence and classified as a high-severity quality concern.

LumenAI converts the frontline finding into a structured executive workflow:

Evidence captured
-> Quality issue classified
-> Severity and disposition generated
-> Alert workflow triggered
-> Vendor intelligence updated
-> QA review visibility created
-> Executive dashboard reflects risk state

## Demo Talking Points

### 1. Evidence Becomes Intelligence

LumenAI does not treat evidence as a static image or attachment. It converts evidence into structured operational intelligence.

### 2. Risk Becomes Action

High-risk findings can be routed into alert queues, QA review, CAPA workflows, vendor escalation, and executive visibility.

### 3. Leaders See the System View

The command dashboard shows risk volume, alert status, vendor patterns, review readiness, and model-performance indicators.

### 4. Vendor Accountability

Instrument quality issues can be grouped by vendor, instrument type, issue category, severity, repeat finding, and escalation pathway.

### 5. Audit Readiness

The platform creates a defensible chain from evidence to action, supporting infection prevention, quality leadership, vendor conversations, and survey readiness.

## Executive Close

LumenAI closes the gap between frontline quality findings and executive governance. It helps regulated healthcare teams see risk sooner, route it correctly, document it defensibly, and close the loop with evidence.
