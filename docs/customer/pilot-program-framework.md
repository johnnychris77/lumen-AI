# LumenAI Pilot Program Framework
Version 1.0

## Pilot Philosophy
Pilots are 90-day, outcomes-focused engagements designed to prove measurable
value before full contract commitment. LumenAI pilots are structured to succeed
— we align on success criteria before day 1 and manage to outcomes weekly.

---

## Pilot Tiers
| Type | Duration | Price | Target |
|------|----------|-------|--------|
| Free Discovery Pilot | 30 days | $0 | 1 facility, Starter features, max 500 inspections |
| Success-Based Pilot | 90 days | $0 upfront; convert at list price | 1 facility, Professional features |
| Paid Pilot | 90 days | 50% of list price | Enterprise/Health System |

---

## Pilot Objectives (Agreed Pre-Start)
1. Demonstrate AI detection accuracy >= 85% agreement with SPD educator review
2. Show measurable throughput improvement (target: >= 15% reduction in inspection time per instrument)
3. Validate baseline comparison accuracy against manufacturer IFU
4. Generate pilot regulatory readiness report (JC/AAMI self-assessment score)

## ROI Measurement During Pilot
Track and document:

1. **Labor savings**: (Pre-pilot inspection time − Post-pilot inspection time) × FTE rate
   - Measure using Digital Twin throughput dashboard
   - Target: 15–20% time reduction per instrument

2. **Instrument savings**: Near-miss instruments caught by AI × estimated replacement cost
   - AI findings of crack/corrosion on instruments not yet in repair queue
   - Value: $500–$5,000 per instrument depending on type

3. **Audit prep savings**: Hours saved on accreditation prep × staff rate
   - Compare pre-pilot manual prep time vs. LumenAI-generated audit package time
   - Target: 60% reduction in prep hours

4. **Avoided events**: Escalations resolved before patient contact
   - Each avoided instrument-attributable SSI: estimated value $25,000–$250,000
   - Document any critical findings caught during pilot

## Pilot-to-Contract Conversion Playbook

### Week 8: Commercial Conversation Begins
- Present early pilot KPI data to SPD Director and CFO
- Identify expansion scope: additional facilities, tier upgrade
- Begin drafting commercial proposal

### Week 10: Pilot ROI Report Delivered
- Generate executive-ready ROI report using CFO dashboard data
- Include 3-year projection with conservative, base, and optimistic scenarios
- Compare LumenAI cost vs. total quantified value delivered

### Week 11: Executive Presentation
- Present to COO/CFO/CNO (30-minute executive briefing)
- Introduce reference customer (peer hospital on Professional or Enterprise)
- Present Professional or Enterprise contract proposal
- Offer multi-year discount as close lever (10% for 2-year, 15% for 3-year)

### Week 12: Contract Decision
- Contract signature or clear no-go with documented reason
- If no-go: document reason; offer Free Discovery tier to maintain relationship
- If partial (single facility only): accept and plan expansion within 6 months

### Post-Pilot Options
| Decision | Next Step |
|----------|-----------|
| Full conversion | Execute contract; begin standard onboarding |
| Partial conversion | Single-facility contract; expansion roadmap agreed |
| Delay (3 months) | Hold pilot data; schedule executive re-engagement |
| No conversion | Free tier offered; reason documented; quarterly re-engagement |

## Pilot Governance

### Weekly Check-In Agenda (30 minutes)
1. KPI scorecard review (10 min)
2. Open issues and blockers (10 min)
3. Adoption update — active users, inspection volume (5 min)
4. Next week plan (5 min)

### Pilot Escalation Path
- Technical issues: CSM → Engineering (4-hour SLA during pilot)
- AI accuracy concerns: CSM → Clinical team review (48-hour SLA)
- Commercial concerns: AE → VP Sales (same day)

### Pilot Termination Criteria
Pilots may be paused if:
- Customer fails to complete training by Week 4
- IT/security issue blocks user access beyond 5 business days
- Customer requests pause for internal budget/prioritization reasons

LumenAI reserves the right to end a free pilot if fraud, misuse, or competitive
intelligence gathering is suspected.
