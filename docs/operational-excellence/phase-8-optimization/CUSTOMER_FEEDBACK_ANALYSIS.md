# LPR-DIR-019 — Customer Feedback Analysis (Phase 8)

## ⚠️ Status: NO CUSTOMER FEEDBACK — PLATFORM NOT LAUNCHED

Customer feedback requires **real customers using a live deployment**. **No
production launch has occurred and none is authorized** (Phase 6: GO WITH
CONDITIONS, production withheld; Phase 7: NOT LAUNCHED; 1 CRITICAL + 8 HIGH
blockers open). There are **no pilot customers, no support tickets, no NPS/CSAT
survey responses, and no feature requests from real users.** All such data below
is therefore **NOT AVAILABLE and is not fabricated.**

## What *does* exist (real — customer-facing assets, not feedback)

The repository contains substantial **customer-enablement documentation** (Phase 5
`SUPPORT_READINESS.md`): onboarding playbook, SPD champion guide, go-live plan,
success checklist/playbook, executive-sponsor guide, and in-product Sage learning
content. These are **inputs** to a future customer relationship — they are **not
feedback** and carry no satisfaction signal.

## Feedback-instrumentation gap (blocks measurement even after launch)

- **CFB-01 (MEDIUM):** No feedback-intake pipeline (tickets, surveys, in-product
  prompt, feature-request board). Even a controlled pilot would generate no
  structured feedback today.
- **CFB-02 (MEDIUM):** No support desk → engineering escalation (ties Phase 5
  SUP-02 / OPS-INC-01), so a pilot's qualitative signal would have no route to the
  backlog.
- **Recommendation (V1.1 candidate):** stand up a minimal, PHI-free feedback loop
  (in-product feedback widget + survey + feature-request intake + triage into the
  backlog) *before* any controlled pilot, so pilot learning is captured honestly.

## Determination

**No customer feedback can be reported.** Both prerequisites are missing: (a) real
customers on a live deployment (blocked), and (b) feedback instrumentation (not
built). This document is a **feedback-readiness framework**, not a satisfaction
record. Any Phase 8 "voice of customer" conclusions must wait for a controlled
launch with instrumented feedback.
