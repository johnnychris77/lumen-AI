The CAPA Predictive Risk Scorecard Frontend Cards make the v1.1 CAPA risk prioritization layer visible to users.

- Overall CAPA risk status
- Average risk score
- High-priority and overdue CAPA counts
- Critical and watch CAPA counts
- Top CAPA risk items
- Recommended actions
- Executive next steps

This advances LumenAI from CAPA governance reporting into executive-ready CAPA risk prioritization.
