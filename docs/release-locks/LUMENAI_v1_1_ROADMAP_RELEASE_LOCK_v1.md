# 4. v1.1 Product Objectives Locked

## 4.1 Governance Intelligence Layer

Goal:
Add intelligence over audit, CAPA, and vendor governance signals.

Planned capabilities:
- Executive risk summary
- Governance health scoring
- CAPA risk prioritization
- Vendor risk prioritization
- Open issue aging analysis
- High-risk signal detection
- Recommended leadership actions

---

## 4.2 Predictive CAPA Risk Scoring

Goal:
Create a CAPA scoring layer that identifies which CAPAs are most likely to become overdue, high-risk, or escalation-worthy.

Potential endpoint:
GET /api/capa/risk-scorecard

Potential fields:
- capa_id
- risk_level
- status
- owner
- due_date
- days_to_due
- is_overdue
- is_high_risk
- escalation_risk_score
- recommended_action
- executive_priority

---

## 4.3 Vendor Performance Scorecard

Goal:
Move Vendor Governance from event tracking into vendor performance scoring.

Potential endpoint:
GET /api/enterprise/vendor-governance/performance-scorecard

Potential metrics:
- total_vendor_events
- high_risk_events
- repeat_events
- capa_linked_events
- unresolved_events
- average_days_open
- vendor_risk_score
- vendor_performance_status

---

## 4.4 Executive Governance Action Queue

Goal:
Enhance the Executive Governance Dashboard from summary reporting into prioritized executive action.

Potential endpoint:
GET /api/enterprise/executive-governance/action-queue

Potential capabilities:
- Audit action queue
- CAPA action queue
- Vendor action queue
- High-risk issue list
- Upcoming due items
- Overdue items
- Recommended executive actions

---
