The purpose of this scorecard is to evaluate whether LumenAI’s Power BI pilot analytics outputs are useful, understandable, ingestible, and valuable for enterprise customer reporting.

It supports:

- Power BI export validation
- Data dictionary validation
- Dashboard field mapping

- Executive reporting validation
- Reporting cadence alignment
- Evidence-backed analytics decisions
- Commercial conversion planning

# 3. Scoring Model

Each validation category should be scored from 1 to 5.

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 5 | Strongly validated |
| 4 | Validated with minor changes |
| 3 | Partially validated |
| 2 | Weakly validated |
| 1 | Not validated |

Recommended interpretation:

| Average Score | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 4.5 to 5.0 | Power BI pilot analytics strongly validated |
| 3.8 to 4.4 | Power BI analytics validated with refinement |
| 3.0 to 3.7 | Continue analytics discovery and field mapping |
| 2.0 to 2.9 | Significant analytics gaps remain |
| Below 2.0 | Power BI analytics not validated |

# 4. Power BI Validation Categories

## Category 1 — Export Structure Readiness

Question:
Can the customer’s analytics team understand and use the LumenAI export structure?

Evidence sources:
- CSV export review
- Analytics team feedback
- Data field review
- Sample dataset review

Scoring guidance:
- 5: Export structure is clear and readily usable
- 4: Export structure is usable with minor changes
- 3: Export structure is partially usable
- 2: Export structure requires significant redesign
- 1: Export structure is not usable

# 7. Power BI Validation Questions

Ask the analytics/BI team:

1. Is the export structure usable for Power BI ingestion?
2. Are field names clear?
3. Are field definitions clear?
4. What fields are missing?
5. What fields are unnecessary?
6. What dashboard pages would be most useful?
7. What filters are required?
8. What refresh cadence is realistic?
9. What data boundary is acceptable?
10. What would make this analytics model scalable?

Ask executive stakeholders:

1. Which metrics would you want to see monthly?
2. Which dashboards would support governance meetings?
3. Which indicators should trigger leadership review?
4. Which reports are currently manual?
5. What would make the Power BI output valuable enough to expand?

# 8. Decision Thresholds

## Strongly Validated

Criteria:
- Average score 4.5 or higher
- BI team acceptance score 4 or higher
- Executive reporting value score 4 or higher
- Data boundary fit score 4 or higher

Recommended action:
Include Power BI analytics in commercial proposal or expanded pilot.

---

## Validated With Refinement

Criteria:
- Average score 3.8 to 4.4
- BI team accepts general model
- Field or dashboard refinements are manageable

Recommended action:
Refine export structure and continue pilot validation.

---

## Continue Analytics Discovery

Criteria:
- Average score 3.0 to 3.7
- Customer sees potential but needs more field mapping or reporting alignment

Recommended action:
Continue BI discovery and revise scorecard after updates.

---

## Significant Analytics Gaps

Criteria:
- Average score 2.0 to 2.9
- BI team has major concerns or executive reporting value is unclear

Recommended action:
Reassess Power BI scope before commercial discussion.

---

## Not Validated

Criteria:
- Average score below 2.0

Recommended action:
Do not position Power BI analytics as a validated commercial driver.
