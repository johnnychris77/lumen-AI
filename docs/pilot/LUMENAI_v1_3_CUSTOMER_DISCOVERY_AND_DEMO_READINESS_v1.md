Thank you for meeting with us. The purpose of this discussion is to understand how your organization currently manages sterile processing quality visibility, CAPA oversight, vendor accountability, and executive reporting.

LumenAI is designed to help leaders move from reactive reporting into predictive governance intelligence by connecting quality events, CAPA trends, vendor performance signals, and Power BI-ready analytics.

2. What quality events are most difficult to trend or escalate?

5. How are vendor quality issues documented and reviewed?

# 8. Objection Handling Guide

## Objection: We already have dashboards.

Response:
Most dashboards show point-in-time metrics. LumenAI focuses on governance intelligence by connecting CAPA trends, vendor patterns, executive recommendations, and evidence-backed decision support.

## Objection: We cannot use PHI in a pilot.

Response:
The initial pilot can use demo data, de-identified examples, synthetic data, or aggregated governance categories. The pilot is designed to validate workflow value without requiring PHI ingestion.

## Objection: We already use Power BI.

Response:
LumenAI is not replacing Power BI. It prepares governance data, data dictionary structure, and export-ready datasets that can feed Power BI reporting.

## Objection: Vendor management is handled elsewhere.

Response:
LumenAI supports vendor accountability by surfacing repeat events, high-risk recurrence, CAPA linkage, and executive review signals that can support existing vendor governance meetings.

## Objection: We need security review first.

Response:
v1.3 includes a security, privacy, and compliance readiness track that will define data boundaries, access expectations, pilot environment assumptions, and customer review needs.

## Objection: We are not ready for full implementation.

Response:
The v1.3 pilot is structured as a limited validation effort, not a full production implementation. The purpose is to confirm value, workflow fit, and next-step requirements.

- Customer identifies relevant stakeholders
- Customer sees value in CAPA trend intelligence
- Customer sees value in vendor trend intelligence
- Customer understands Power BI export readiness
- Customer asks about pilot scope, data, implementation, or pricing
- Customer agrees to a follow-up discovery or pilot planning session
