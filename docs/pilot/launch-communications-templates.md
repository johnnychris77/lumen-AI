**From:** [Site Coordinator]  
**Subject:** New Quality Tracking Tool — LumenAI Starts Today

---

Team,

Starting today, we're piloting LumenAI — a digital tool to help us track instrument quality after decontamination.

**What this means for you:**

- After each decontamination visual inspection, log the result in LumenAI (takes about 2 minutes)
- You'll need your work email and the password [Coordinator] will give you
- The system records what you found — staining, cracks, corrosion, etc.
- No patient information goes into this system — instruments only

**To get started:**
- Open [LumenAI URL] in Chrome or Edge
- Sign in at /login
- The training guide is posted in [break room / Teams channel / shared drive]

**Why we're doing this:**  
This helps us identify patterns in instrument quality over time so we can address issues before they become problems.

Questions? See [Coordinator Name] or email [CS Email].

[Facility Name] has selected LumenAI for a quality intelligence pilot, and we'd like to include baseline reference data for your instruments.

**What we're asking:**

Please submit baseline reference images and specifications for the following instruments:  
- [Instrument list from site coordinator]

**How to submit:**

1. Navigate to [LumenAI URL]/vendor-intake
2. Fill in your company and product details
3. Upload reference images (JPEG or PNG, up to 10 MB each — clean instrument, standard lighting)
