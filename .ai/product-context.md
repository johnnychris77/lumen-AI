# LumenAI Product Context

## What LumenAI Is
LumenAI is an AI-powered inspection and workflow intelligence platform for sterile processing and instrument reprocessing operations.

## Current Product Direction
LumenAI is evolving from a working MVP into an investor-ready, enterprise-credible platform.

## Core Product Capabilities
- Inspection upload
- Async job queue
- AI inference
- Inspection history
- PDF reporting
- Reviewer workflow
- Artifact tracking

## Target Users
- Sterile Processing leaders
- Reprocessing managers
- Infection prevention stakeholders
- Healthcare operations leaders
- Quality and compliance teams

## Product Principles
- Traceable outputs
- Reliable async processing
- Reproducible deployment
- Enterprise architecture
- Measurable workflow value

## Business Goal
Position LumenAI as an investable AI operations platform with a path to enterprise deployment and strategic healthcare adoption.
