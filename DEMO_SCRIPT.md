# LumenAI Demo Script

## Demo Goal

Show LumenAI as an executive intelligence and governance platform that turns operational risk into board-ready action.

## Opening Positioning

LumenAI is an enterprise AI-ready operating layer for regulated healthcare and customer-success environments. It connects risk detection, executive insight, remediation tracking, governance escalation, KPI trend monitoring, auditability, and role-based access control into one command center.

## Demo Flow

### 1. Health and readiness

Show:

- `/api/health`
- `/api/production-readiness/config`

Message:

LumenAI has a production-readiness check that validates environment configuration, public URL, scheduler settings, RBAC, audit, SMTP/webhook readiness, and deployment issues.

### 2. Executive Dashboard

Open:

`http://127.0.0.1:18011/api/executive-briefing-dashboard/view`

Show these sections:

- Tenant Executive Insights
- Tenant Remediation Workflow
- Executive Escalation & Governance Cadence
- Governance Packet Exports & Board Delivery
- Executive KPI Trends
- Automated KPI Scheduler
- Executive Decision Log
- Enterprise Audit Trail
- Enterprise RBAC Policy Guardrails

### 3. Tenant Risk Intelligence

Explain:

LumenAI converts tenant data into risk drivers, executive summaries, board attention flags, and recommended actions.

### 4. Closed-loop Remediation

Show:

- Create Actions from Tenant Insight
- Start / Escalate / Close

Message:

Risk is not just reported. It becomes trackable remediation work with owners, status, due dates, and escalation.

### 5. Executive Escalation

Show:

- Run Escalation Scan
- Open Executive Escalations
- Acknowledge / Close
- Create Decision

Message:

The platform identifies overdue, blocked, escalated, and critical actions that need executive review.

### 6. Governance Packet

Show:

- Generate + Export + Deliver Governance Packet
- DOCX / PPTX / PDF links

Message:

LumenAI turns governance data into board-ready artifacts with delivery audit history.

### 7. KPI Trends

Show:

- Capture KPI Snapshot
- Current vs Previous Snapshot Movement
- Executive Trend Narrative

Message:

Leaders can see whether risk is improving, worsening, or stable over time.

### 8. Audit and RBAC

Show:

- Audit Events
- Access Decisions
- Policy Matrix

Message:

Every action can be monitored, and role-based policy guardrails protect governance-sensitive workflows.

## Closing Statement

LumenAI demonstrates an enterprise operating system pattern: risk detection, insight generation, accountable action, escalation, executive decision logging, board reporting, auditability, and access governance.
