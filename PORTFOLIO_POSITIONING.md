# LumenAI Portfolio Positioning

## One-line Pitch

LumenAI is an enterprise executive intelligence platform that converts operational risk into board-ready insight, accountable remediation, governance escalation, KPI trend reporting, and audit-ready decision tracking.

## Strongest Portfolio Angle

This project demonstrates the ability to combine:

- healthcare operations knowledge
- enterprise governance workflows
- executive reporting
- AI-ready narrative generation
- DevOps and Docker deployment
- API design
- dashboard development
- audit and access control patterns
- automated quality gates

## Target Roles This Supports

- Healthcare AI Product Manager
- Applied AI Solutions Architect
- Healthcare Operations Transformation Leader
- Technical Program Manager
- Data/Analytics Product Owner
- Enterprise Workflow Automation Consultant
- Customer Success Operations / Governance Leader

## Interview Story

I built LumenAI to demonstrate how regulated operations can move beyond static dashboards. The platform turns risk signals into executive narratives, remediation actions, escalations, governance packets, KPI trends, decision logs, audit events, and RBAC-controlled workflows.
